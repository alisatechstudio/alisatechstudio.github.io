1. Introduction

Alisa Tech Studio builds mobile applications for Android and iOS. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), Google Play's User Data policy, and Apple's App Store Review Guidelines (including Privacy Nutrition Labels and App Tracking Transparency requirements).

This Privacy Policy explains what information our apps collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices available to you. By downloading, installing, or using any app published by Alisa Tech Studio, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

7. Tracking, Advertising & App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

If any of our apps use advertising or cross-app/cross-website tracking, we comply with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. On iOS 14.5 and later, we request your permission via the system prompt before tracking you or accessing your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) for tracking purposes. You may change this permission at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

On Android, you may reset or opt out of the use of your Advertising ID for personalized ads via your device's Settings > Privacy > Ads menu.
